Reday

Measure your satisfaction, not your mood.

Reday doesn't measure how you feel — it measures if your daily decisions were aligned with who you want to be. Inspired by stoicism: what you control is what you do, not what you feel.

Why Reday exists

Mood-tracking apps measure what you don't control: how you feel. Reday flips it — at the end of the day you answer one question: "did you make decisions aligned with who you want to be?". Applied stoicism: we evaluate action, not mood.

01

One question

No long forms. One tap a day to log whether it was an aligned day or not.

02

Year view

Each day a dot. Patterns visible across weeks, months, years. See your life at a glance.

03

Optional journal

If you want to note why, there's a free-text field. If not, the tap is enough.

04

Statistics

Streaks, percentages, trends. Data to understand your own behavior.
