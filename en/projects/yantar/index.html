Your kitchen, organized.

Recipe websites full of ads, infinite scroll, and pop-ups. Yantar extracts the clean recipe from any URL and saves it offline, ready to cook.

Why Yantar exists

Cooking should be simple. But recipe websites have become an ad battlefield: kilometric stories, pop-ups, autoplay videos. Yantar removes the noise: paste the URL, get the clean recipe, save it offline, ready to use in cooking mode without distractions.

01

Clean extraction

Paste any recipe URL and Yantar gives you only the essentials: ingredients and steps.

02

Offline-first

Your recipes live in local SQLite. Cook without connection, ads, or tracking.

03

Cooking mode

Big steps, screen always on, no interruptions when your hands are busy.

04

Collections

Organize recipes by style, diet, or occasion. Find what you cook with a single tap.
